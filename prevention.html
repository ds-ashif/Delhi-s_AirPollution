Causes of Delhi Air Pollution

Delhi's air pollution is caused by a variety of factors, including:

  • Industrial emissions
  • Vehicle exhaust
  • Construction dust
  • Stubble Burning in Punjab and Haryana
  • Domestic and commercial fuel burning

Effects of Delhi Air Pollution

The health effects of Delhi's air pollution are significant and include:

  • Respiratory problems, such as asthma and bronchitis
  • Cardiovascular disease
  • Cancer
  • Low birth weight in infants
  • Impaired cognitive development in children

Prevention of Delhi Air Pollution

Some of the ways we can prevent Delhi's air pollution include:

  • Reducing vehicle emissions through public transport and electric vehicles
  • Implementing cleaner industrial practices
  • Encouraging waste management and composting to reduce burning of agricultural waste
  • Promoting use of clean energy sources such as solar power
